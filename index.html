About Cuppa

Cuppa is more than a coffee shop; it's a celebration of India's rich coffee heritage. From the aromatic plantations of Coorg to the bustling streets of Mumbai, we bring you the authentic taste of Indian coffee, crafted with love and tradition. Every cup tells a story of the land, the people, and the centuries-old rituals that make Indian coffee unique.
